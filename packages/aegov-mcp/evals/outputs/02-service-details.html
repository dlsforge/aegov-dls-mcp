Payment of a traffic fine

دفع مخالفة مرورية

Pay outstanding traffic fines issued in the UAE quickly and securely. Sign in with UAE Pass to review the fines linked to your traffic file, vehicle plate or driving licence and settle them online. A payment receipt is issued instantly.

About this service

The “Payment of a traffic fine” service allows UAE residents and visitors to view and settle traffic violations recorded against their traffic file, vehicle plate number or driving licence. Fines may be issued for offences such as speeding, illegal parking, running a red signal, or failing to renew vehicle registration.

Once you sign in with UAE Pass, the service retrieves all outstanding fines linked to your identity in real time. You can pay a single fine, selected fines, or all fines at once, and download an official payment receipt immediately after the transaction is confirmed.

Paying certain fines within the discount period may qualify you for a reduction. The applicable discount, if any, is shown automatically before you confirm payment.

The process

  1. 1 Sign in
  2. 2 Review fines
  3. 3 Pay
  4. 4 Receipt
  1. 1

    Sign in with UAE Pass

    Authenticate using your UAE Pass digital identity. This confirms who you are and links the service to your traffic file securely.

  2. 2

    Review your outstanding fines

    The service displays every fine linked to your identity, including the offence, date, location and amount. Select the fines you wish to pay.

  3. 3

    Complete the payment

    Pay securely using a credit or debit card, Apple Pay, or your linked bank account. Any eligible discount is applied automatically before confirmation.

  4. 4

    Download your receipt

    Once payment is confirmed, an official receipt is issued instantly. A copy is also sent to your registered email and saved to your account.

Conditions of use

Before using this service, please ensure the following conditions are met:

  • You must hold a valid UAE Pass account verified to at least the basic (SOP1) level.
  • The fine must be registered and finalised in the traffic system. Fines still under review cannot be paid until confirmed.
  • Fines subject to an active objection must have the objection resolved before they can be settled.
  • Payment must be made using a payment method issued in your name or by an authorised representative.

Service specifications

Completion duration

Immediate — the transaction completes as soon as payment is confirmed.

Service fees

No service fee. You pay only the value of the fine (AED), less any applicable discount.

Service channels
  • Government website
  • Smart app (iOS and Android)
  • Customer happiness centres
Payment channels
  • Credit / debit card (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Direct debit from a UAE bank account
Target audience

Individuals, vehicle owners, drivers and companies with registered vehicles in the UAE.
