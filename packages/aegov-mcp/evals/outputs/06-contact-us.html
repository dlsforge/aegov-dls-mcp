Contact us

We are here to help. Send us your enquiry using the form below, call us on our toll-free number, or visit the Ministry during official working hours. We aim to respond to all enquiries within two working days.

Send us a message

We require your email address to respond to your enquiry. We will never share your email with anyone else.

Enter your number without the country code, for example 55 1234567.

Please include any reference numbers related to your enquiry. Do not include your Emirates ID number or other sensitive personal information in the message.
